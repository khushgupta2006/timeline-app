1576: Battle Of Haldighati

A major conflict fought on June 18, 1576, between the forces of Maharana Pratap of Mewar and the Mughal Empire under Akbar. It took place at the Haldighati mountain pass in the Aravalli Range, Rajasthan. However the results weren't decisive but mughals failed to capture Mahrana Pratap.
